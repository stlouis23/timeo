About

Art can be a powerful tool against slavery and its legacy. Through various artistic mediums, art can raise awareness, amplify voices, empower individuals and communities, and challenge stereotypes. This website aims to promote the use of art in the fight against slavery and its legacy.

Raising Awareness

Visual mediums such as paintings, sculptures, and murals can be used to depict the brutal realities of slavery and the injustices that continue to exist today.

Amplifying Voices

Artistic mediums such as poetry, music, and dance can be used to amplify the voices of those impacted by slavery, providing a platform for self-expression and storytelling.

Empowering Individuals and Communities

Art can empower individuals and communities by fostering creativity and promoting healing. It can provide a sense of agency and hope for those impacted by slavery.

Challenging Stereotypes

Art can challenge stereotypes and shift the narrative around slavery and its legacy, promoting greater understanding and empathy.
